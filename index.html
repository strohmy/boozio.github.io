Vodka Martini

This classic cocktail is the height of sophistication, loved by true cocktail aficionados the world over, shaken or stirred.

  • light splash of vermouth
  • 3 oz vodka
  • 3 olives

Splash vermouth into glass and swirl to coat. In a shaker, combine vodka with ice and shake vigorously. Strain into glass. Garnish with olives on a toothpick.
